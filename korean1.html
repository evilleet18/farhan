My Little Bride is a 2004 South Korean romantic comedy film about an arranged marriage between a female high school student,(Moon Geun-young) and a male college student (Kim Rae-won).

it was the second most popular domestic film at the Korean box office in 2004

  • IMDB Rating: 7.1/10
  • Directed by: Kim Ho-jun
  • Produced by: Choi Soon-shik
  • Written by: Yoo Soon-il
  • Starring: Moon Geun-young,Kim Rae-won
  • Music by: Choi Soon-shik,Choi Man-shik
  • Cinematography: Seo Jeong-min Edited by Park Soon-deok
  • Distributed by: Korea Pictures
  • Release date: April 2, 2004
  • Running time: 115 minutes
  • Country: South Korea
  • Language: Korean


***My Little Bride is a Korean remake of the 2002 Hong Kong film My Wife Is 18 ***
